LD2450 Radar → CP2102 Wiring

LD2450 RADAR             CP2102 USB ADAPTER
────────────             ──────────────────

■ Black (5V)   ──────────►  ■ Red (VOUT)

■ Yellow (GND) ──────────►  ■ Black (GND)

■ White (TX)   ──────────►  ■ Yellow (RXD)

■ Red (RX)     ◄──────────  ■ Blue (TXD)
⚠️ LD2450 COLORS ARE COUNTERINTUITIVE!
• Black = 5V (not ground!)
• Yellow = GND (not signal!)
Trust the connector labels, not the colors.
CP2102 Pin Order (VOUT → CTS): 
Red    - VOUT (power)     ← USE
Black  - GND              ← USE
Blue   - TXD (transmit)   ← USE
Yellow - RXD (receive)    ← USE
Orange - DTR              (not used)
Green  - CTS              (not used)

Baud Rate: 256000
